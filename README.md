# 1. Who has more bones in their body?

- Cats -Correct Answer
- Humans
- Rabbits
- Hamsters

Cats have more bones than humans. Cats have approximately 230 - 250 bones, while an adult human has 206.

# 2. How much of a cat's life is spent sleeping?

- 1/4
- 1/2
- 1/3
- 2/3 -Correct Answer

Cats spend around 12 to 16 hours of their day sleeping; this high amount of rest is due to an evolutionary trait designed to conserve energy for hunting.

# 3. Which Cat breed is the largest?

- Ragdoll
- Maine Coon -Correct Answer
- Siamese
- Balinese

The Maine Coon is commonly considered to be the largest domesticated Cat breed.

# 4. Who invented the Cat flap?

- Freddie Mercury
- Sir Isaac Newton -Correct Answer
- Benjamin Franklin
- Charles Darwin

Sir Isaac Newton is commonly credited with inventing the Cat flap in order to prevent his Cats from interrupting light-sensitive experiments by scratching at his door.

# 5. whose heart beats faster?

- Cats
- Humans
- Dogs
- Hamster -Correct Answer

A Cat's heart rate ranges from 140 to 220 beats per minute, while a hamster has a significantly faster resting heart rate, commonly ranging between 250 and 600 beats per minute.

# 6. On average, how long do Cats live?

- 5 years
- 10 years
- 15 years -Correct Answer
- 25 years

On average, indoor Cats usually live between 12 and 20 years old. Outdoor cats typically don't live as long.

# 7. What is a group of Cats called?

- Clawers
- Clowder -Correct Answer
- Cloup
- Clapers

A group of Cats is known as a clowder.

# 8. What is the average speed of a house cat?

- 0 - 6 mph
- 7 - 13 mph
- 14 - 19 mph
- 20mph and above

The average speed for most house cats is somewhere around 20 miles per hour; however, a cat's speed may vary depending on their fitness, breed, health, age, etc.
