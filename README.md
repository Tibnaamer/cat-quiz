# Questions and Answers

WIP 8 Questions to Use for my quiz

## 1. Who has more bones in their body?

- Cats -Correct Answer
- Humans
- Rabbits
- Hamsters

_Cats have more bones than humans. Cats have approximately 230 - 250 bones, while an adult human has 206._

## 2. On average, how many hours a day do cats sleep?

- Less than 4 hours
- 5 - 11 hours
- 12 - 16 hours - Correct Answer
- More than 16 hours

_Cats spend around 12 to 16 hours of their day sleeping; this high amount of rest is due to an evolutionary trait designed to conserve energy for hunting._

## 3. Which domesticated Cat breed is the largest?

- Ragdoll
- Maine Coon -Correct Answer
- Siamese
- Balinese

_The Maine Coon is commonly considered to be the largest domesticated Cat breed._

## 4. Who is commonly credited with inventing the Cat flap?

- Freddie Mercury
- Sir Isaac Newton -Correct Answer
- Benjamin Franklin
- Charles Darwin

_Sir Isaac Newton is often credited with inventing the cat flap, although this is likely a myth; it is believed he made it in order to prevent his Cats from interrupting light-sensitive experiments by scratching at his door._

## 5. Whose heart beats faster?

- Cats
- Humans
- Dogs
- Hamster -Correct Answer

_A Cat's heart rate ranges from 140 to 220 beats per minute, while a hamster has a significantly faster resting heart rate, commonly ranging between 250 and 600 beats per minute._

## 6. On average, how long do Cats live?

- Around 5 years
- Around 10 years
- Around 15 years -Correct Answer
- Around 25 years

\_On average, indoor Cats usually live between 12 and 20 years old. Outdoor cats typically don't live as long.

## 7. What is a group of Cats called?

- Clawers
- Clowder -Correct Answer
- Cloup
- Clapers

_A group of Cats is known as a clowder._

## 8. What is the top speed of a house cat?

- 0 - 6 mph
- 7 - 13 mph
- 14 - 19 mph
- 20mph and above -Correct Answer

_The top speed for most house cats is around 20 miles per hour; however, a cat's speed may vary depending on factors such as fitness, breed, health, and age._
