1. Who has more bones in their body?

Cats -Correct Answer
Humans

Cats have more bones than humans. cat's have approximately 230 - 250 bones, while an adult human has 206.

1/2
1/3
2/3 -Correct Answer

Cats spend around 12 to 16 hours of their day sleeping, this high amount of rest is due to an evolutionary trait designed to conserve energy for hunting.

Sir Isaac Newton -Correct Answer

5. whose heart beats faster?

Cats -Correct Answer
Humans

A Cat's heart rate ranges from 140 - 220 beats per minute, while a normal resting heart rate for a human is between 60 - 100 beats per minute.
